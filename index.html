Learn the story behind CarBnR and the inspiration that brought it to life.

The inspiration for CarBnR struck during a memorable road trip across the country with friends. We envisioned a car rental experience that was as exciting and carefree as the journey itself - one that eliminated the hassle and offered a diverse selection of vehicles to fuel our wanderlust. Fueled by this dream, I embarked on creating CarBnR, a web app that embodies the spirit of adventure and empowers travelers to explore new horizons with ease.

This project not only served as a personal challenge but also fulfilled a requirement for my Portfolio Project at Holberton School. I poured my heart and soul into developing CarBnR, and I'm thrilled to share it with the world.
